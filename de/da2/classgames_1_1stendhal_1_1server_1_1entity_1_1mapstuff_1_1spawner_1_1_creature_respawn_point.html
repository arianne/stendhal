Detailed Description

RespawnPoints are points at which creatures can appear. Several creatures can be spawned, until a maximum has been reached (note that this maximum is usually 1); then the RespawnPoint will stop spawning creatures until at least one of the creatures has died. It will then continue to spawn creatures. A certain time must pass between respawning creatures; this respawn time is usually dependent of the type of the creatures that are spawned.

Each respawn point can only spawn one type of creature. The Prototype design pattern is used; the prototypeCreature will be copied to create new creatures.

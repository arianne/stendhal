Detailed Description

Checks the records of kills. Quest string should have in proper quest index string like "creature1,w,x,y,z,creature2,a,b,c,d,creature3,..." Where creature1, creature2 - names of creatures to kill; w,x and a,b - number of creatures to kill, solo and shared; y,z and c,d - number of creatures killed by player before starting this quest, both solo and shared.

void onLoggedIn (Player player)
 

Detailed Description

Implementing classes can be notified that a player has logged in.

After registering at the LoginNotifier, the LoginNotifier will notify it about each player who logs in.

It is the responsibility of the LoginListener to determine which players are of interest for it, and to store this information persistently.

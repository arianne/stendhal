void onLoggedOut (Player player)
 

Detailed Description

Implementing classes can be notified that a player has logged out.

After registering at the LoginNotifier, the LoginNotifier will notify it about each player who logs out.

It is the responsibility of the LogoutListener to determine which players are of interest for it, and to store this information persistently.
